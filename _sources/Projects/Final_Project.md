# Final Project - Comparison of Two Entities
*Check Canvas for Deadlines and Office Hours*

**Goal:** Compare two similar but distinct entities (products/things/services/software). 

You choose the two entities and *measure* them (see an example below). Be creative and choose every day things from your life (try not to spend money). 

**Conditions on the choice:**

For each item, the sample size must be greater than 4 and less than 30. 

**Key Question to Answer:** 

*Based on the measurements, are the two entities similar?* You are the statistician, so use the concepts, tools, and techniques discussed in class to provide an answer like a statistician.


**An Example** 
(*This is just an example. Please come up with your own entities and measurements. Be creative and try not to spend money*)

I want to know if organic apples and *normal* apples are distinct in terms of its weight (*measure*). I may buy about five of each (randomly) from the same Publix store and measure the weight of each with a kitchen weight scale (and perhaps eat them after measuring). Now I have the data ($2 \times 5$ observations) that I can use to answer the question!
